# Marketing Growth Codex

Use this to turn vague growth goals into channel-specific execution.

## Intake

Capture what is known:

- Product, offer, and audience.
- Current stage: pre-launch, early traction, growth, scale.
- Goal: leads, signups, revenue, retention, authority, launch.
- Budget, team size, timeline, constraints.
- What has already worked or failed.

## Strategy Router

- **Need leads fast**: paid search, LinkedIn, cold email, partner lists, sales enablement.
- **Low budget**: SEO, founder-led content, communities, social proof, comment marketing.
- **Authority**: thought leadership, podcasts, webinars, reports, book/consulting funnel.
- **Product-led**: onboarding, referral loops, powered-by loops, upgrade prompts.
- **Conversion**: landing page messaging, pricing, paywall, signup flow, proof, objections.
- **Launch**: waitlist, early access, Product Hunt, email sequence, partner amplification.

## Output Standard

Recommend 3-5 ideas unless the user asks for a full plan. For each:

- Why it fits.
- First steps.
- Assets needed.
- Expected result.
- How to measure.

For campaigns, provide copy, audience, channel, timing, CTA, and success metric.
