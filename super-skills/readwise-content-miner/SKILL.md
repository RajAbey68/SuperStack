# Readwise Content Miner

Use this to turn saved attention into content ideas.

## Workflow

1. Establish the window: last 24 hours, 3 days, week, month, or topic filter.
2. Pull saved items from Readwise/Reader or user-exported highlights.
3. Cluster items by theme, tension, audience pain, or repeated claim.
4. Generate content ideas with source links attached.
5. Rank ideas by fit, novelty, audience relevance, and ease of execution.
6. If useful, convert the workflow into a daily/weekly automation.

## Output Format

For each idea include:

- Idea/title.
- Format: short, thread, video, post, newsletter, lead magnet.
- Why it matters.
- Source link(s).
- Suggested hook.
- Execution note.

## Quality Rules

- Always include original source URLs when available.
- Separate the saved author's claim from our interpretation.
- Do not overfit to one tweet or highlight; look for patterns.
- If source URLs are missing, mark them as unavailable.

## Automation Pattern

Good recurring prompt:

```text
Every morning, review my Readwise/Reader saves from the last 3 days and produce 10 content ideas with source links, hooks, and a top-3 recommendation.
```

## Source Notes

Distilled from the user-provided creator/marketing skills transcript: use Readwise as a second brain for daily content ideation and always preserve original links.
