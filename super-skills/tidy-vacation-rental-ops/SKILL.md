# TIDY Vacation Rental Ops

Use this for short-term rental operational workflows.

## Turnover Workflow

1. Intake booking calendar, checkout/check-in times, property address, unit type, and special guest notes.
2. Identify same-day turns and late checkout/early check-in conflicts.
3. Assign cleaners by availability, travel distance, property fit, and reliability.
4. Produce task checklist, inspection requirements, linen plan, consumables restock, and photo proof.
5. Escalate maintenance issues that block guest readiness.
6. Confirm completion before guest arrival.

## Cleaner Dispatch Output

For each job, include:

- Property and access notes.
- Checkout and check-in deadline.
- Cleaner assignment.
- Priority level.
- Room-by-room checklist.
- Linen and consumables.
- Required photos.
- Escalation contact/path.

## Operating Rules

- Same-day turnovers get explicit time buffers.
- Guest-impacting defects are urgent even if cheap to fix.
- Do not promise readiness without completion evidence.
- Keep guest communications warm and professional, without mentioning internal chaos.
