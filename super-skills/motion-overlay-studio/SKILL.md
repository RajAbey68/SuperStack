# Motion Overlay Studio

Use this for creator/business motion graphics, especially overlays and product demos.

## Workflow

1. Identify target platform: YouTube intro, launch video, short-form overlay, app demo, product demo, or B-roll.
2. Choose engine based on availability:
   - Remotion for mature React/video composition workflows.
   - Hyperframes for fast motion graphics and physics-style animations.
3. Define composition, scenes, duration, aspect ratio, and export format.
4. Reuse existing templates when possible.
5. Generate 2-4 variants when exploring style.
6. Inspect screenshots/previews and fix overlap, timing, text density, and motion.
7. Render/export final video.

## Editing Language

Useful instructions:

- "At 8 seconds, zoom in on the phone."
- "At 11 seconds, zoom out."
- "Make the first scene fly in from the left."
- "Turn the gradient red at 10 seconds."
- "Make this message splat onto the screen."
- "Create four scenes in one composition so I can choose."

## Quality Rules

- Keep on-screen text short.
- Use visual rhythm: entrance, hold, emphasis, exit.
- Avoid excessive motion that distracts from the point.
- Verify the rendered video exists and opens.
- Provide the final file path.
