# Sales Pipeline Codex

Use this for practical sales work, especially consulting and advisory pipelines.

## Sales Workflow

1. Identify deal stage: lead, qualified, discovery, proposal, negotiation, closed/won, closed/lost, dormant.
2. Capture buyer, pain, urgency, budget, decision process, next step, and risks.
3. Prepare the next interaction with a clear objective and questions.
4. Create concise follow-up that confirms value, commitments, owner, and date.
5. Forecast based on evidence, not optimism.

## Qualification

Use a light MEDDICC-style check when appropriate:

- Metrics: quantified business impact.
- Economic buyer: who owns budget.
- Decision criteria and process.
- Identified pain.
- Champion.
- Competition and status quo.

## Outputs

- **Call prep**: account context, likely pains, questions, proof points, objection plan.
- **Follow-up**: recap, value summary, actions, date/time, CTA.
- **Forecast**: stage, probability, amount, close date, confidence, risks, next action.
- **Competitive intel**: customer need, alternatives, differentiation, landmines.
