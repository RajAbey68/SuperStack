# Blog Codex

Use this for full-lifecycle blog work.

## Router

- **Strategy**: audience, positioning, topic clusters, cadence, distribution.
- **Brief**: search intent, reader problem, angle, outline, sources, internal links, CTA.
- **Write**: answer-first structure, evidence, examples, clear headings, useful conclusion.
- **Rewrite**: preserve truth, improve clarity, update stale claims, strengthen intro/CTA.
- **Audit**: score content quality, search fit, structure, sources, freshness, schema, and AI citation readiness.
- **Repurpose**: convert to LinkedIn posts, email, webinar, sales one-pager, or short-form script.
- **Fact-check**: verify numbers and claims against high-quality sources.

## Quality Rules

- No fabricated statistics or uncited factual claims.
- Use direct, answer-first paragraphs under H2s.
- Keep paragraphs generally under 150 words.
- Do not skip heading levels.
- Add descriptive image alt text when images are used.
- Use Tier 1 sources for legal, financial, medical, technical, or time-sensitive claims.

## Output Standard

For a new post, include:

- Working title and search intent.
- Outline or complete draft as requested.
- Source plan or citations when research is involved.
- SEO title/meta suggestion when useful.
- Repurposing hooks if the user wants distribution.
