# Brand Deal Manager

Use this for sponsorship inbox triage and creator partnership operations.

## Workflow

1. Define time window: today, 72 hours, past week, or custom.
2. Search email for sponsorship, paid promotion, partnership, influencer, affiliate, launch, and collaboration language.
3. Deduplicate brands and threads.
4. Research each company enough to judge fit.
5. Score priority:
   - Audience fit.
   - Budget likelihood.
   - Brand quality.
   - Product relevance.
   - Urgency.
   - Past relationship.
6. Produce a table or spreadsheet.
7. If requested, inspect calendar availability and propose call times.
8. Draft replies only after user approval.

## Table Columns

- Brand/company.
- Contact.
- Ask.
- Offer/budget clues.
- Fit score.
- Priority.
- Research notes.
- Recommended next action.
- Suggested meeting windows, if requested.
- Source email/thread reference.

## Rules

- Do not send emails without explicit user approval.
- Do not expose private email content in reusable skills.
- Prefer high-quality fit over raw volume.
- Be concise: the goal is fast decision-making.
- Flag suspicious, low-quality, or unclear offers.

## Automation Pattern

Good recurring workflow:

```text
Every morning, scan sponsorship outreach from the last 24 hours, produce a prioritized table, and email or save the summary for review.
```
